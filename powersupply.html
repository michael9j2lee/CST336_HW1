Power Supply

The power supply is used to store all of the electricity from the socket

It is recommend that users use a power strip that protects against shortages or electric spikes

Each power supply will be able to maintain a certain level of wattage.

Each power supply connects to all of the components in your computer which includes:

  • MotherBoard
  • Video Card
  • Hard Drives
  • Fans

This unit is extremely important.
